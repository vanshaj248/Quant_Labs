# Quantitative Finance Career Roadmap
## July 2026 – March 2027 (Final Year)

> **Goal:** Advanced quant skill-building + MBA applications (CAT 2026, GRE, XAT) targeting IIMs, ISB, and international programmes for a career in Quant Finance / Asset Management / Risk.

---

## 🎯 Overall Strategy

- Complete capstone quant project and publish on GitHub
- Sit CAT 2026 (Nov) and GRE (Dec 2026 / Jan 2027)
- Apply to IIMs, XLRI (XAT), ISB (EEO), and GRE-based international programmes
- Prepare for WAT-PI rounds (Jan–Mar 2027)
- Grow LinkedIn network to 150+ quant/finance professionals

---

## 🗓️ Monthly Roadmap

---

### 📅 July – August 2026: Advanced Quant + ML Finance + Exam Kickoff

#### Quant Skills
- **Topics:** GARCH models, regime-switching, CVaR/Expected Shortfall, Copula models, Alternative data
- **Book:** *Advances in Financial Machine Learning* – Marcos López de Prado
- **Course:** Quantitative Methods for Finance – NYU (free audit)
- **ML:** XGBoost, LightGBM, LSTM for financial time series
- **Project:** Begin capstone — full asset allocation engine (Python + Streamlit UI)

#### CAT 2026 — Kickoff
- Start: 2 hrs/day
- Topics: Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry (QA fundamentals), RC basics, DILR intro
- Resources: IMS, TIME, Career Launcher (online modules)

#### GRE — Diagnostic Phase
- Take a full-length diagnostic test (ETS PowerPrep — free)
- Identify weak areas in Quant and Verbal
- Begin: 15 words/day vocabulary habit
- Target: Quant 165+, Verbal 155+ (320+ total)

---

### 📅 September – October 2026: Capstone + Exam Acceleration

#### Projects
- Complete capstone: MPT optimiser + Sharpe/Sortino analytics + Streamlit UI
- Publish 2 blog posts on Medium or LinkedIn from project learnings
- Participate in QuantInsti or Kaggle finance challenge
- Push all code to GitHub with clean READMEs

#### CAT 2026 — Intensive Phase
- Ramp up to 4–6 hrs/day
- Complete 15+ full-length mocks by October 31
- Analyse every mock for error patterns (don't just take mocks — review them)
- Focus areas: DILR set accuracy, RC speed, QA shortcuts

#### GRE — Structured Prep
- Resources: Manhattan Prep 5lb book (Quant), Magoosh (Verbal + AWA)
- 2 full mocks/week
- AWA: practice timed essays (30 min Analyse an Issue, 30 min Analyse an Argument)
- Book your test slot (December or early January) — register at least 3 weeks early

---

### 📅 November 2026: CAT Exam + MBA Research

#### CAT 2026 — Final Week + Exam Day
- Final week: light revision only — shortcuts, RC strategies, DILR frameworks
- No new topics in the final 7 days
- Rest well the night before

#### Post-CAT: MBA Programme Research
| Target (CAT) | Target (XAT/GRE) |
|---|---|
| IIM A – PGP / PGP-ABM | XLRI BM / HRM (XAT) |
| IIM B – Finance track | ISB PGP – EEO |
| IIM C, IIM L, MDI Gurgaon | NUS, HKUST, Nanyang (GRE) |

#### Networking Push
- Connect with MBA graduates in quant finance / asset management on LinkedIn
- Attend: CFA Society India events, IIQF webinars, QuantInsti workshops
- Target: 150+ meaningful LinkedIn connections by year-end

---

### 📅 December 2026: GRE Exam + SOP Drafting

#### GRE — Final Prep & Exam
- 2 full mocks in the first week of December
- Sit the GRE (December or early January slot)
- Score valid 5 years — useful for ISB, INSEAD, NUS, LBS
- Target: **320+ total**

#### MBA Applications — SOP Draft
- Draft Statement of Purpose: quant background → MBA → asset management / risk roles
- Identify 2–3 recommenders (professors, internship managers, mentors)
- Compile: resume, transcripts, certifications, GitHub/blog links

#### XAT Preparation (for XLRI)
- XAT typically held on January 5 each year
- Unique section: **Decision Making** — practice 5+ XAT-specific mocks
- XLRI BM and HRM are top choices for quant + finance profiles

---

### 📅 January – February 2027: MBA Applications + WAT-PI Prep

#### Applications to Submit
- **CAT-based:** IIM A/B/C/L/K, MDI Gurgaon (calls release Jan–Feb)
- **XAT-based:** XLRI, IMT Ghaziabad, XIMB
- **ISB:** Early Entry Option (EEO) — if eligible before graduation
- **GRE-based international:** NUS Business School, HKUST, Nanyang Business School

#### WAT-PI Preparation
- Key themes: Why MBA, Why now, Career goals (quant finance), Extracurriculars
- Daily reading: Economic Times / Mint (markets, policy, business news)
- WAT practice: 250 words in 20 minutes on current affairs / abstract topics
- Mock PIs: practice with seniors/peers from target IIMs

#### Parallel: Final Year Academics
- Maintain strong GPA — IIM PIs scrutinise academic record
- Complete any pending certifications (Python, Bloomberg Market Concepts, Risk)

---

### 📅 March 2027: Interviews + Final Decisions

#### PI Season
- IIM Personal Interviews typically happen in March–April
- Prepare your "quant finance story": projects → internship → MBA → career goals
- Be ready for stress questions: defend your GPA, project choices, career rationale

#### Comparing Admits
- Evaluate: placement records, finance specialisation depth, alumni network, fees vs ROI
- If no IIM A/B/C: IIM L/K, MDI Gurgaon, XLRI are strong for finance careers
- ISB and international decisions typically arrive March–April

#### Final Quant Profile Polish
- GitHub: 4+ projects, clean READMEs, live demo links
- LinkedIn: 150+ connections, 5+ posts, recommendations from mentors
- Resume v3: internship + projects + exam scores + blog links
